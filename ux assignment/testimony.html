Student Testimonies

President's Testimony (2023–2024)

Fuaad Al-habib Yeo

"I can confidently say that my journey in Outdoor Adventure Club (ODAC) was one of the best times in my life. ODAC has changed my lifestyle completely in an amazing way.

In Sec 1, I was an introvert and unsure if I made the right choice. But from the moment I joined, I was welcomed with open arms. ODAC was not just a CCA — it was a family. Each session included outdoor skills, fitness, and bonding games.

These experiences taught me first-aid, knot tying, kayaking, orienteering, and rock climbing — helping me overcome fears and build confidence. Becoming President wasn’t easy, but I was supported by my peers. I’ve grown in ways I never expected.

“Know that the only impossible journey is the one you never begin.”

President 2023, Fuaad Al-habib Yeo (bottom right)

Vice President's Testimony (2023–2024)

Leandro Leow Shao Hng

"ODAC has given me a rare opportunity to lead as Vice President of Programme. This experience has been a spectacular journey, and I hope the fun will not stop even after I graduate.

Although it was hard to adapt to ODAC's high physical demands, my seniors were kind and patient, giving me advice to improve and enjoy the CCA fully.

Leading a large CCA can be tough, but I enjoy planning activities and seeing them come to life. Camps and competitions made me feel included and allowed me to grow.

“Success is no accident. It is hard work, perseverance, learning, studying, sacrifice and most of all, love of what you are doing or learning to do.” – Pele

Vice-President (Programme), Leandro Leow Shao Hng
